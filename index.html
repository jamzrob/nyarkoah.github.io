About Us

Cooky’s Catering Services provides professional catering solutions for our client’s everyday events. We offer the traditional local Ghanaian dishes as well as the colourful, unusual contemporary international dishes for all kinds of occasions upon the client’s request. We also make fruit juices, smoothies and traditional drinks as well. We are committed in providing healthy meals and hence use natural spices and fresh ingredients at all times. We especially avoid the use of items with artificial preservatives and monosodium glutamate extracts to ensure that our meals are wholesome.
